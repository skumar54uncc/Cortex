Welcome to Cortex

Cortex keeps a private, on-device memory of readable pages you visit so you can search and ask questions over what you actually read.

Right now Cortex is also scanning the last 7 days of your browser history (same behavior as Settings → History scan) and indexing open tabs in the background. Pause/blocklist and sensitive-site skips still apply.

  1. Browse normally — pages are chunked and indexed locally (respecting your pause/blocklist).
  2. Press Ctrl+Shift+K (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+K (Mac), or Alt+Shift+C, to open Search / Ask / Digest on any page.
  3. Use the toolbar icon for stats and Open settings for privacy and optional cloud chat.
